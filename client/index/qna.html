Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create an account?

    Our logins are based on Microsoft accounts, so please create a Microsoft account.

  • How many entries can I have?

    Unlimited!

  • What cloud services are supported?

    Currently, we support Dropbox, OneDrive and Google Drive.

  • Can I edit the generated card post if I think it is inaccurate?

    Sure! At the bottom of each post, there will be an icon for you to do so.

  • How does the application work?

    The application looks at the new food pictures you uploaded into your cloud (i.e. Dropbox etc) and analyse the picture to automatically create a card post.

  • Is my data safe and secure?

    Yes, we only store the data which we require such as the pictures to generate the card post. When you delete your account, our server automatically wipes all the data associated with your account.
